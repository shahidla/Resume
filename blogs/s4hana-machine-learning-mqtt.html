SAP S/4HANA, Machine Learning, and MQTT

Originally published on SAP Community

This article explored combining SAP S/4HANA, machine learning, and MQTT messaging to connect enterprise business processes with event-driven and predictive capabilities. The work fits into a broader theme of exposing SAP processes to lightweight messaging and intelligence patterns.

Technical theme

The prototype connected SAP application logic with messaging-driven interaction. MQTT provides a lightweight publish-subscribe model, while machine learning adds a decisioning or classification layer that can support business process automation.

Resume relevance

This work demonstrates early exploration of SAP, machine learning, and real-time messaging patterns that later informed event-driven BTP, CAP, Integration Suite, and AI workflow prototypes.
